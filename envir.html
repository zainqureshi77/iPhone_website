Our disassembly robots — Daisy, Dave,

used to make new ones.



Apple Trade In.

When you’re ready to buy a new iPhone, you can
trade in your current iPhone or Android device and apply any
credit toward your purchase. If your device isn’t eligible for credit,
we’ll recycle it for free.
